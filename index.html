How accounts work

Each Google Sheet has its own Apps Script URL. To switch to a different Google account (or a different spreadsheet), just deploy a new Apps Script in that sheet and paste the new URL above — then click Save & Connect. Your local cache will clear and the app will reload data from the new sheet.
